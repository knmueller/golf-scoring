Help

Entering Scores

To enter scores for you and your partner, go to the Home page and enter your scores as they happen. Once you enter a score for a hole or holes, you can click the Submit button on that page to submit your scores. Your scores will be saved and you will see them every time you visit the Home page.

Viewing Results

To view results for everyone and where you stand against the competition, go to the Results page. You will see 4 tables -- Front 9, Back 9, Total 18, and a Team Net. Enter your scores as you go during your round to see real-time stats!

Registering Your User

All usernames are already preloaded. The username consist of the first letter of your first name followed by your last name. For example, a username for "Kyle Mueller" would be kmueller.
For first time login, head to the Registration page and set a password. You will be redirected to the login page and you should now be able to login. Once logged in, you will have the ability to enter scores for all the players on your team.

Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password or need to reset it, please contact Kyle. He will be able to reset the password, and you can then re-register your user.
